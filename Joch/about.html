About Us

The team at JoCh Analytics and Consult is driven by a passion for data, research, and policy analysis. Founded by John Osman Froko and Christopher Mbiba, both former research assistants at the University of Cape Coast, our mission is to provide insightful analytics and consulting services that empower businesses and organizations to make informed decisions.
